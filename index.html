Hi, I'm Peter!

3 First Names Design is the doppelgänger for my work as a Product & User Experience Designer.

I’m currently looking to join a team who want to take on big challenges.

Some people call designers like me a “hybrid designer”, and I’ve never been a fan of that label.

We’re designers who have experience in UX, UI, and Visual Design, and we might even sprinkle in a little code too - which I do. That was never the goal when I started my career in Design; these were skills and experiences born out of necessity.

No front-end support? Okay, I’ll learn CSS. No data or research on who we’re designing for? Fine, I’ll call some people who might be interested in this product.

If you’re hungry and diligent about stacking days-on-top-of-days, you discover you can improvise, adapt, and overcome any design challenge thrown your way. I’ve built my career on that foundation, and it’s served me well as I’ve navigated a range of products and work environments. From eCommerce to video games; from small start-ups to Fortune 500 companies.

To me, that’s what makes a Product Designer: someone who can see the entire chess board of a project’s design and contributing elements. I’m someone you can hand-off a vague goal, work independently and collaboratively within a team, and to come out the other end with a solution.

Let’s blaze some trails!
